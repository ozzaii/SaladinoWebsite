Our Story

From our humble beginnings to becoming a premier travel agency, discover the passion and commitment that drives Saladino Travel.

Contact UsView Tours

Bringing the Magic of Turkey & Dubai to Life Since 2010

Saladino Travel was born from a deep love for the rich cultures, stunning landscapes, and historic wonders of Turkey and Dubai. Our founder, Marco Saladino, first visited Istanbul as a backpacker in 2005 and was instantly captivated by the city where East meets West.

After spending years exploring every corner of Turkey and later Dubai, Marco assembled a team of passionate locals who shared his vision: to create immersive travel experiences that go beyond the typical tourist routes and connect travelers with the authentic heart of these extraordinary destinations.

Today, Saladino Travel has grown into a premier travel agency, yet we remain true to our original mission. Our hand-crafted tours combine must-see highlights with hidden gems, creating journeys that transform mere tourists into true travelers who connect deeply with the places they visit.

Our Values Guide Everything We Do

Every tour, every interaction, and every decision is shaped by our commitment to these core values.

Authentic Experiences

Sustainable Tourism

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact and supporting local communities through responsible tourism practices.

Personalized Service

No two travelers are alike. We tailor our services to match your interests, preferences, and travel style for a truly personalized journey.

Cultural Respect

We approach every destination with respect for local customs, traditions, and ways of life, promoting cross-cultural understanding.
